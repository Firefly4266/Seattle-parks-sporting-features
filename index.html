About the App

Have you ever planned to play sport with friends and not known where your nearest field was? Or how about trying to find any sporting field when you move to a new city? Field Finders uses the most up to date data from the City of Seattle to connect you to your sporting field of choice. Up for a swim? Want to play basketball? Have you ever even heard of Pickleball? Seattle Field Finders will connect you to a huge range of different city run sporting facilities located all round the city. Start searching and get out onto the field
